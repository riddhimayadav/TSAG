Mission Statement

The Texas Sports Analytics Group (TSAG) aims to leverage advanced data analytics and research methodologies to provide actionable insights and strategic recommendations within the business of sports through three key pillars:

01

Consulting

Partner with real sports organizations to solve challenges in fan engagement, performance, and business strategy. Turn data into impact, both on and off the field.

02

Research

Dive into player efficiency, team metrics, and the data behind the game. Build insights that fuel portfolios, research, and real-world results.

03

Education

Learn the tools of the trade through weekly technical workshops. Connect with industry pros, coaches, and faculty in our exclusive speaker series.
